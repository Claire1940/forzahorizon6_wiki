### Quick tuning workflow

1. Pick one car class and one test route.
2. Run 3 baseline laps with current settings.
3. Change only one variable (braking line, traction aid, steering style).
4. Run 3 more laps and compare average time and error count.
5. Keep changes that improve consistency, not just one fast lap.

This method is especially useful for **Forza Horizon 6 xbox one** players because hardware limits reward clean, repeatable driving more than aggressive inputs.

> **Warning:** Making too many tuning changes at once can hide what actually improved your lap time.
