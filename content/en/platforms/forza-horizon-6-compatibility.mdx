## Common Compatibility Problems and Fixes

Even a good setup can hit launch-week friction. Use this quick matrix before reinstalling anything.

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Quick Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Game launches but stutters in dense areas** | Over-aggressive visual preset or background apps | Lower heavy settings, close overlays, retest route |
| **Wheel feels numb or twitchy** | Deadzone/FFB mismatch | Reset profile, calibrate center, retune in small steps |
| **Can’t join friends** | Account permission/NAT issues | Recheck privacy + multiplayer settings, restart session |
| **Audio sounds flat or uneven** | Device output mismatch | Confirm stereo/spatial mode and in-game dynamic range |
| **Frequent disconnects in convoy** | Wi-Fi instability or region mismatch | Use wired connection, recreate convoy, retry region later |

> **Tip:** Keep one “safe” preset for online racing and one “quality” preset for solo exploration. Switching profiles is faster than full troubleshooting every session.
