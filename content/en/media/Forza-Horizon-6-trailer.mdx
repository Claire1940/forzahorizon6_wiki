## How to Analyze Future Reveals Like an Editor (Not a Hype Machine)

Most players watch a trailer once and jump straight to conclusions. You’ll get better results by using a repeatable review process. Follow these steps whenever new **Forza Horizon 6 trailer** footage appears:

2. **Track what’s explicitly shown**

3. **Separate technology from style**  
   Color grading and camera cuts can make visuals look dramatic. Wait for raw driving sequences before judging handling feel.

4. **Cross-check platform messaging**  
   Watch official posts from Xbox, Forza channels, and developer statements before trusting repost accounts.

5. **Score confidence by category**  
   Use “High, Medium, Low” confidence tags for map, career progression, online modes, and accessibility features.

| Analysis Category | What to Look For | Common Mistake | Better Approach |
