## FAQ

**Q: Is Forza Horizon 6 pre order worth it if I only want the base game?**

A: If you only care about base content and do not need early access, Standard is usually the most cost-efficient route. You still get the full core experience, and you can add DLC later if your playtime grows.

**Q: What is the main Forza Horizon 6 pre order bonus in 2026?**

A: The key listed pre-order reward is a pre-tuned Ferrari J50 variant. Some stores may also run limited promotional perks, but those are platform and timing dependent.

**Q: Should I get Deluxe or jump straight to Premium?**

A: In many cases, Premium gives better long-term value because it adds VIP perks, car packs, and two expansions for a moderate price increase over Deluxe. Deluxe fits best if you only want Car Pass + Welcome Pack.

**Q: Can Game Pass players get early access without buying the full game?**

A: Yes, typically through the Premium Add-Ons Bundle path. But keep in mind: add-ons do not replace game ownership if your subscription ends.
