## How to Claim Your Veteran Gifts

Claiming your **Forza Horizon 6 loyalty rewards** does not happen the instant you press "Start." The developers have integrated the reward delivery into the narrative flow of the 2026 festival. Follow these steps to ensure you don't miss out:

1.  **Complete the Prologue:** You must finish the initial driving sequence and the first major race event.
2.  **Unlock the First Hub:** Once you arrive at the main Festival site and receive your first starter car, the system will begin the check.
3.  **Check the Message Center:** Navigate to the "My Horizon" tab in the pause menu. Select "Message Center" and then "Gifts."
4.  **Download Rewards:** Highlight each loyalty message and press the "Download" button to add the car to your garage.

## Troubleshooting Missing Rewards

It can be frustrating when your **Forza Horizon 6 loyalty rewards** fail to appear. This is a common issue during the 2026 launch phase due to high server loads. If you haven't received your cars after two hours of gameplay, consider the following checklist:

*   **Account Matching:** Are you using the exact same Gamertag that has the previous save files?
*   **Cross-Platform Sync:** If you played *Forza Horizon 5* on Steam but are playing the new title on Xbox, you must ensure your [official Xbox Profile](https://www.xbox.com) is linked to your Steam account.
*   **Privacy Settings:** Ensure that "Share Game Data" is enabled in your account privacy settings, otherwise, the game may be blocked from "seeing" your previous titles.

| Issue | Potential Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **No Message in Inbox** | Restart the game and ensure you are connected to Horizon Life (Online). |
| **Missing Specific Car** | Check if the previous game save is actually on the cloud and not just local. |
| **Server Error** | Wait 24 hours; during launch weeks, gift delivery can be delayed. |

## FAQ

**Q: Can I still get Forza Horizon 6 loyalty rewards if I play the old games now?**

A: Yes! In most cases, if you go back and create a save file in *Forza Horizon 5* or *Motorsport* today, and then return to the new game, the system will eventually detect the new save and send your rewards. However, this may take up to 48 hours to process.

**Q: Do I need to own the games, or is Game Pass enough?**

A: Game Pass is perfectly sufficient. As long as you have launched the game and created a save file while the subscription was active, you are eligible for the **Forza Horizon 6 loyalty rewards**.

**Q: Are there rewards for playing non-Forza games?**

A: Generally, no. The loyalty program is strictly limited to the Forza ecosystem (Horizon and Motorsport). There are occasionally promotional crossovers with other Xbox Game Studios titles, but these are handled as separate DLC or "Gift" drops rather than loyalty rewards.

**Q: What happens if I accidentally deleted my gift car?**

A: Unfortunately, loyalty gifts are usually a one-time grant. If you delete the car from your garage, the support team rarely replaces it unless there was a documented technical error. Always "Lock" your favorite loyalty cars in the garage menu to prevent accidental deletion.
