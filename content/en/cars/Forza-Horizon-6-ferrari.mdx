## Performance Tuning for S1 and S2 Classes

To stay competitive in the Horizon Open and Trial events, you must optimize your **Forza Horizon 6 ferrari** for specific PI (Performance Index) ceilings. Most Ferraris start at the high end of A class or the middle of S1. Pushing a car into S2 requires a careful balance of weight reduction and tire compound selection.

### S1 Class Optimization (Grip Focus)
For S1, the goal is usually to keep the car under the 900 PI limit while maximizing lateral G-force. The Ferrari 458 Speciale and the 488 Pista are the preferred choices here. 

1. **Tire Compound**: Use Semi-Slick Race Tires for the best balance of PI cost and grip.
2. **Drivetrain**: Keep it Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD) to save weight, unless you struggle with throttle control.
3. **Weight**: Prioritize the Race Weight Reduction upgrade to improve flick-ability in chicanes.

### S2 Class Optimization (Speed Focus)
In S2 (998 PI), you are competing against the fastest cars in the world. The SF90 Stradale and the FXX-K Evo are your primary weapons.

| Upgrade Component | Recommendation | Impact on Handling |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Front Wing** | Forza Race Wing | **High** - Increases turn-in response |
| **Rear Wing** | Carbon Fiber Active | **Extreme** - Prevents high-speed oversteer |
| **Transmission** | 7-Speed Race | **Medium** - Allows for fine-tuned gear ratios |
| **Brakes** | Carbon Ceramic | **High** - Drastically reduces stopping distance |

> **Warning:** Avoid over-stiffening the suspension on your **Forza Horizon 6 ferrari**. The 2026 map features various road imperfections that can cause a stiff car to bounce, leading to a loss of traction.

## Mastering the Mid-Engine Handling

Driving a Ferrari in FH6 requires a different technique than driving a front-engine muscle car or an AWD hatchback. Because the weight is concentrated in the middle, these cars rotate very quickly.

*   **Trail Braking:** Gently let off the brakes as you turn into the corner to keep weight on the front tires.
*   **Throttle Application:** Wait until the car is pointed at the exit before pinning the throttle. Mid-engine cars can "snap" into a spin if you are too aggressive with the gas while the wheels are turned.
*   **Differential Settings:** Setting your Acceleration Differential to around 40-50% can help manage the power delivery out of slow corners.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best Forza Horizon 6 ferrari for drag racing?**

A: The Ferrari SF90 Stradale is currently the top contender for drag racing due to its hybrid AWD system and incredible launch stats. When tuned correctly, it can compete with the fastest hypercars in the game over a quarter-mile.

**Q: How do I unlock the Ferrari 250 GTO in 2026?**

A: The 250 GTO is typically a "Barn Find" or a high-tier Accolade reward. In the 2026 version of the game, it requires completing a series of classic racing challenges in the "Historical Apex" campaign branch.

**Q: Can I change the engine in my Ferrari?**

A: While engine swaps are available for many cars, most Ferraris have limited options to maintain their brand identity. However, you can often upgrade the existing engine with twin-turbo conversions or race-grade internals to significantly boost horsepower.

**Q: Why does my Ferrari feel slidey on dirt roads?**

A: Most Ferraris are designed for tarmac. If you must take a **Forza Horizon 6 ferrari** off-road, you need to install Rally Suspension and Off-Road Race Tires to compensate for the low ground clearance and stiff chassis.
